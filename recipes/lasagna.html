Lasagna Dominicana

Description

Beef lasagna is a favorite for special occasions, so of course it's going to be present on a Christmas dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ready-to-bake lasagna noodles
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheese (mozzarella or cheddar)

    • For tomate sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 tablespoon oregano
  • 4 cups ripe tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup grated carrot

Steps

  1. Pour the oil into a deep pot, add the onion, and sauté for 3 minutes. Add the garlic and tomato paste, then stir in the ground beef and mix constantly until the beef is no longer lumpy. Add the tomatoes, oregano, and salt, and cook for another 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and set aside.

  2. For the red sauce: combine all the ingredients in a pot and cook for 15 minutes, or until thickened.

  3. In a casserole dish (or an aluminum baking dish), pour the red sauce. Place lasagna sheets on the bottom and cut them into pieces to cover the edges. Continue layering with meat, cheese, and red sauce. Repeat this process until you have used both sauces and topped with cheese.

  4. Cover the dish with parchment paper, making sure it doesn't touch the cheese. Bake covered at 350°F for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake for another 10 minutes in a water bath. Remove from the oven and let cool before serving.
