Ensalada de papas

Description

This potato salad,called Ensalada rusa in the DR, combines potatos with carrots, peas,onions,eggs with mayonnaise. Some people like to add other ingredients like beets, raisins or apples.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound potatoes
  • 2 large carrots
  • 3 egg (medium)
  • 2 teaspoons salt, or more, to taste (divided)
  • 1 red apple
  • 1 medium red onion
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • ½ cup green peas, blanched or canned
  • ½ cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boiling

    2. Place the potatoes, carrots, and eggs in a large pot and add enough water to cover them (and extra). Add a tablespoon of salt. After 12 minutes have passed, remove the eggs from the water and continue boiling the carrots and potatoes until they are cooked through.

  2. Peeling and dicing

    3. Once cooled to room temperature, peel potatoes, carrots, eggs, apple, and dice into small cubes.

  3. Prepare the onion

    4. Mince the onion, add vinegar and let it rest until it's time to mix with the rest of the ingredients (at least 10 minutes). Discard the vinegar.

  4. Mix

    5. Mix potatoes, carrots, eggs, apple, onion, corn, and peas. Add mayonnaise and mix. Season with salt to taste.

  5. Serve

    6. Keep salad chilled at all times until it's time to consume unless you'll serve it shortly after finishing it. You can either serve chilled or at room temperature, depending on your preferences.
