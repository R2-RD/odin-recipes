Cerdo Asado

Description

Puerco asado, lechon asado or pernil, however you wanna call it, it's the center of a Dominican Christmas dinner.To most people, the christmas dinner would not be complete without it.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup pitted green olives
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 head garlic, large, peeled
  • 4 tablespoons oregano (dry, ground)
  • 2 tablespoons pepper (freshly-cracked, or ground)
  • 2 cubanela (cubanelle pepper), or green bell pepper, diced
  • ⅓ cup lime juice, or sour orange juice
  • 7 tablespoons salt, for the seasoning and 3 to go directly to the pork meat
  • 15 pound pork, [6.7 kg] (bone-in leg of ham, or shoulder)
