Odin Recipes

Recipes for a traditional Dominican Christmas dinner

Like most hispanic countries, Dominicans celebrate Christmas eve, Nochebuena, with a big dinner. Here's a list of recipes so you can have your Christmas Dinner Dominican style.

  • Moro de guandules
  • Lasagna
  • Pastelon de platano maduro
  • Cerdo Asado
  • Ensalada de papas
  • Pasteles en hojas
